We exist to see Heaven transform Austin

We're a passionate community of belivers that are sold out for the Kingdom. We believe that Christ laid down His life for us and as us. We believe that we are called to spread the Kingdom. We believe that the Church is a movement and we're committed to partnering with God to see His dream come true in Austin.

Unite

We unite the body of Christ to focus our efforts on long-term results for specific areas of need (e.g., sex industry, homeless community).

Equipping

We host events with powerful teaching and activations that are designed to equip the saints to BE the Church.

Community Service

We host events to raise money for the purpose of serving our city as well as mobilize people to do the work involved to beautify the city.

Worship Gatherings

We gather together for the purpose of worshipping Jesus and exalting His name.
